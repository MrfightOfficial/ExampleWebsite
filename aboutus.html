Why Did We Start The Sanctuary?

Gifted created the server with the hope of bringing people together with three goals in mind: to find comfort, healing, and lifelong friendships. Within 8 months, The Sanctuary has grown to over 2000 caring and friendly members, each having a safe haven to come to.

Can I join the team?

We are always looking for potential new staff to help us grow! We have positions starting from Welcomer, where you welcome all our newcomers, Listeners who take support sessions and help our member base with life's troubles, and Protectors, who guard the gates of The Sanctuary and ensure all are safe. We are interested in honest, active members, so if you feel you fit the bill, see the Applications channel to find out more!

Why should I join here?

The Sanctuary is a place to call home if you enjoy chatting with people about a plethora of topics, joining in on events and giveaways, joining active voice chats, and speaking with our Listeners about whatever troubles you! We are an all-inclusive 13+ community with a server economy, level based roles, as well as color roles and custom roles purchasable in the shop! 55 five star reviews on Disboard don't lie, come see what The Sanctuary has to offer you today!
